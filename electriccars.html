Electric Cars vs. Petrol Cars

This page is a collection of the research I conducted for a project in CO150. It contains both scholarly articles on the pros and cons of electric cars, as well as popular articles that cover similar topics. All research will be aimed at answering the question: With major car manufacturers moving towards fully electric lineups, what are the environmental and economic impacts, and how will these impacts affect the future of the automobile industry?
